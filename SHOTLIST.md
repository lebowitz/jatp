# JATP Finale Trailer — Shot List
**"The Crossing" (Option D)** · target ~2:30–2:40 · photorealistic · Veo 3 image-to-video

## Format & production notes
- **Aspect:** generate Veo at 16:9, letterbox to 2.39:1 cinemascope in post (ffmpeg).
- **Clip length:** every VEO shot ≤ 8s. ~19 Veo clips + 5 text cards.
- **Consistency:** each shot lists the `refs/storybook/*.png` portrait(s) to pass as Veo reference images, plus a **first-frame still** concept to generate with Nano Banana Pro and seed image-to-video.
- **Audio (locked):** Veo audio discarded. The whole trailer rides **"Don't Let Go"** laid in post. Dialogue is delivered as **TEXT CARDS**, not lip-synced lines — keeps us off Veo's weakest feature.
- **Color:** warm filmic grade, slightly desaturated shadows; the curse/jolt beats get a cold cyan flicker; the crossing-over light is a blooming warm white.

## Song-to-trailer map ("Don't Let Go")
| Trailer section | Song section | Feel |
|---|---|---|
| Act 1 (joy/calm) | piano intro | intimate, sparse |
| Act 2 (threat) | verse 1 under tension / drop out | unease, percussive hits |
| Act 3 (the choice) | pre-chorus build | rising dread + heartbreak |
| Act 4 (farewell perf) | full-band chorus + key change | anthemic, tears-up |
| Act 5 (reversal) | quiet "Stay." outro | breath-held, then button |

ECU on hands: Julie's hand drifts toward Luke's, passes half-through — then **rests solid** on it. Both freeze. Slow rack focus to her astonished eyes.
- refs: julie, luke · first-frame: macro of two hands almost touching, soft bokeh

Julie and Luke, foreheads nearly together, a beat that doesn't resolve. She pulls back, smiling, scared.

- audio: music cuts to a single piano note — the **"Stay."** outro
